添加`xmlns:nl="http://ilyl.life"`声明

```xml
<RepeatButton nl:ButtonAssist.Type="Primary" 
                Content="Primary" 
                Style="{StaticResource RepeatButtonStyle}" />
```

`RepeatButton`添加`RepeatButtonStyle`样式应用，样式效果与`Button`一致，详情请看[Button](./button.md)说明。
